About

I am an Applied Scientist in the Investment Banking line of business at JPMorgan Chase, where I am currently conducting fundamental research on the theory and science of Gradient Boosting and Semi-Supervised Graph models in Fraud Intelligence. I previously earned my Bachelors in Computer Science with a specialization in Big Data, graduating from SRMIST in 2023. My research interests are ML/DL and Natural Language Processing and my interest topics include Probabilistic Reasoning, Sequence Models, Human-Centered AI, HCI, Causality, and Explainability. During my Bachelors, I have worked on Applied NLP at Samsung Research and the Accessible Computing Lab at McGill University.

I am enthusiastically open to collaboration on exciting research that drives innovation. If you're interested in working together or have opportunities available, please feel free to reach out to me!
